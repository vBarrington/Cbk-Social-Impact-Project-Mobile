People Affected

These user groups are affected by the lack of information delivered to the residents in communities concerning what they cannot recycle and what cannot be trash. Whether they work at the recycling center or contribute to adding items to the bin, they are all connected to this issue. These groups are separated by age, education, needs, and concerns. With more information, they can each make better decisions with their waste habits.

User Group 1

People ages 18-40
Education high school or higher
Work in the Recycling and Waste industry making $35,000+
Talks with family members about another occurrence of the number of non-recyclable items that have appeared at the recycling center and have caused issues

User Group 2

People of all ages
Lives in a household of two or more
Lives in a neighborhood that has a scheduled recycling pick-up
Making over $60,000
Wonder how to get rid of their old electronics and broken furniture

User Group 3

Students, ages 8-18
Lives in a household of two or more
Has been learning about landfills and recycling centers
Questions the households recycling habits
