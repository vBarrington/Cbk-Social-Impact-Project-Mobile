Recycling is our World.

Residents do not have enough information to discard items that are considered not trash and non-recyclable. This issue leaves room for these items to be discarded improperly.

Did You Know...

"Wishful recycling" or "aspirational recycling" happens when people mean well, but actually do more harm than good. Your household trash might be near empty, but placing the wrong item in recycling can contaminate the entire pile, and potentially an entire truckload, sending it straight to landfill.

Key Facts

  • China used to be the biggest market for recyclables. They were not picky about what was sent to them since 2017 they have put a limit on what they will take from other countries.
  • There are many people who are interested and in search of where to put items that cannot be trashed or recycled.
  • Some of the material that cannot be recycled can cause contamination to affect other recycable items.
  • E-waste is better given or sold to a person or a company rather than thrown in the recycling.
  • Some communities have handled the cost of recycling by eliminating it all together or put a restriction on what can be recycled.
